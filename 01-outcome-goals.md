# 01 – Outcome Goals

## 💡 Why this exercise matters
Every MVP needs a clear, measurable learning goal.  
Outcome Goals anchor the team on what success means for users and for you as builders.

## 🎯 Expected Outcomes
- Three-week goal focused on user value and learning  
- Shared definition of “done”  

---

### 🔧 Prompt – Write Your Outcome Goal
Use this template:

> **In [time frame], [user type] will [capability] resulting in [measurable result].**

Add a learning goal if relevant:

> **In the same time, our team will learn to [technical or PM skill].**

**Example (user + learning):**  
> In 3 weeks, data analysts will receive AI-generated career matches resulting in 70 % rating them more relevant than current job-search methods, and our team will learn to deploy a complete LLM pipeline end-to-end.  

✅ Checklist: Time-bound • User-focused • Measurable • Teachable
