### 🔧 **CryptoPulse AI: Now-Next-Future Roadmap**

**NOW (Weeks 1–3: The MVP)**
*Goal: Prove the core hypothesis that a transparent, structured signal increases trader confidence.*

*   **Core Signal Engine:** Build the Python backend to ingest BTC data and generate the primary signal (Direction + Probability).
*   **Alignment Matrix & Risk Meter:** Develop the logic to generate the data for the Alignment Matrix and the Volatility Risk Meter. This is the core of our "transparency" feature.
*   **Streamlit Signal Dashboard:** Create the initial user-facing dashboard to display the live signal, matrix, and risk meter in a clear, intuitive way.
*   **Confidence Score Feedback:** Implement the simple 1-5 star feedback mechanism to capture the Perceived Confidence Score (PCS) from our testers.
*   **Initial Deployment:** Deploy the backend and frontend so a small group of test users can access it live.

**NEXT (Month 2: Building Trust & Stickiness)**
*Goal: Enhance the user experience to build credibility and encourage habitual use, based on initial MVP feedback.*

*   **Signal History & Backtest Transparency:** A top priority. Display the performance of the last 20-30 signals to build trust. This directly addresses insights from your research.
*   **"No-Trade" Signals:** Introduce "No Edge – Stand Aside" signals to build immense credibility and prevent users from overtrading.
*   **Basic User Accounts:** Allow users to sign up so we can start tracking retention (Metric M4) and gather more personalized feedback.
*   **Enhanced Analytics:** Start tracking more detailed behavioral metrics, like the Signal-to-Action Rate (M1), to see if users are acting on the signals.

**FUTURE (Month 3+: Expansion & Automation)**
*Goal: Scale the product by adding more assets, features, and automation to serve a broader audience.*

*   **Expand to More Assets:** Add support for ETH and other major cryptocurrencies.
*   **Mobile Push Notifications:** A key feature request from your user research (especially for the "Alex" persona) to drive re-engagement.
*   **Advanced Models & Features:** Explore more complex models (like LSTMs) and introduce new features like a news event calendar or advanced backtesting filters.
*   **Personalized Education Layer:** Build out features for the "Jordan" persona, such as in-app explanations of indicators and model logic.
*   **TradingView Integration:** Explore the possibility of integrating the signals as an overlay directly within the TradingView platform, meeting users where they already are.

This roadmap provides a clear, sequenced plan. The "NOW" is our committed 3-week sprint. The "NEXT" and "FUTURE" sections ensure we don't lose sight of the bigger vision.
